About

What's up!

I'm a computer engineer working and living in West Michigan. Currently I am working with a local start-up known as TwistHDM on an IoT product known as Limelight. I've had a hand in designing/validating as well as developing firmware for testing.

On the side I contribute to my blogftgstudio.com, it's a blog about life, technology, and music.

Being a Michigan native I enjoy everything the Greate Lakes state has to offer. This includes long days at the beach, dir biking, hiking, camping, fishing, hunting, snowmobiling, snowboarding...pretty much anything that involves being outdoors.

When I'm not outdoors I spend my time in my home studio creating all sorts of music. My favorite musicians include Stevie Ray Vaughn, Jimi Hendrix, Eric Clapton, and BB King. You name it and I'll sure as hell try to play it. If I'm not playing music you'll find me at the gym partaking in various circuit and resistance training exercises.

My technical interests include embedded systems, heuristic algorithms, machine learning, and image processing.
